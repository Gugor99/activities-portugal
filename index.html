Check out Portugal's landscapes and wonderful sunsets

Climb your way to the top of the south coast!

The limestone formations in the Algarve provide great conditions for climbing all year round. Most of the rock climbing areas are set up in the National Park. This means that you will get to experience some of the most scenic areas in Portugal! 90% of climbing areas are situated at the cliff, so imagine moving along the azure blue ocean accompanied by the sound of waves and refreshing breeze!
